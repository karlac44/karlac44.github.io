Lighting

Light plays an important role in establishing the significance of a scene

lighting changes the mood in the scene

dark lighting implies that something bad/dark is about to happen or is happening

Yellow/ very bright lighting signifies that something good is happening or is going to happen

The ways that the lights are facing the characters in specific scenes helps show how the characters are feeling in that instance.
